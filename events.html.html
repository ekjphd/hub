EVENTS

Workshops, Speakers, and Community Gatherings

Coming Soon - Spring 2026

The Constellation Hub will launch a comprehensive series of workshops, guest speaker events, and community programs designed to empower students with the skills, knowledge, and networks needed to thrive as leaders across industries.

Planned Workshop Series

Narrative & Documentation Skills

Master the art of capturing and sharing powerful stories through hands-on training in interview techniques and oral history methods.

  • Interview Skills Workshop – Learn professional interviewing techniques, active listening strategies, and how to create a comfortable space for authentic conversations. Practice conducting interviews that draw out compelling narratives and meaningful insights.
  • History Harvest Techniques – Explore community-based archival methods for collecting, preserving, and presenting personal and professional histories. Discover how to ethically document lived experiences and create digital archives that honor diverse voices.

Digital Skills & Technical Literacy

Build essential technical capabilities for creating, managing, and sharing digital content in professional contexts.

  • HTML & Web Development Fundamentals – Gain hands-on experience creating web content, understanding the building blocks of the internet, and developing digital literacy for the modern workplace.
  • GitHub & Version Control – Learn collaborative workflows, project management, and open-source contribution practices used across industries from tech to publishing to education.
  • Digital Storytelling Tools – Explore platforms and techniques for multimodal composition, from interactive timelines to multimedia portfolios that showcase your unique skills and experiences.

Critical AI Literacy

Develop informed perspectives on artificial intelligence and its implications for careers, creativity, and society.

  • Understanding AI in the Workplace – Examine how AI tools are transforming industries and careers. Learn to critically evaluate AI technologies, identify ethical considerations, and position yourself as a thoughtful user and advocate.
  • AI & Creativity – Explore the intersection of artificial intelligence and human creativity. Discuss questions of authorship, authenticity, and innovation in an age of generative AI.
  • Bias, Ethics & Justice in AI – Investigate how AI systems can perpetuate or challenge existing inequalities. Develop frameworks for evaluating and advocating for responsible AI development and deployment.

Guest Speaker Series

The Constellation Hub will host leading women and gender-diverse professionals from across industries who will share their journeys, insights, and advice. These events provide opportunities to hear directly from successful leaders with humanities backgrounds who are making an impact in fields including technology, business, nonprofit leadership, government, media, publishing, and beyond.

Each speaker event will include time for Q&A and networking, creating connections between students and professionals while building a community of support and mentorship.
