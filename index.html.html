CONSTELLATION HUB

Amplifying Narratives Across Space and Time

Our Mission

Creating a digital and physical hub for the collection, celebration, and preservation of humanities-focused narratives by women and gender-diverse leaders across industries.

The Vision

The Constellation Hub showcases the powerful contributions of women and gender-diverse professionals with humanities backgrounds who are leaders in their fields. Through compelling digital storytelling, strategic industry partnerships, and targeted skills development, this initiative creates lasting cultural change while directly supporting women humanities students in accessing impactful careers.

This addresses the persistent myth that humanities education is incompatible with corporate success—a misconception that disproportionately affects women who are overrepresented in these majors.

What We Do

Through interviews, digital narratives, and collaborative projects, we connect students with professionals across industries to learn about their journeys, challenges, and triumphs. Each story becomes a star in our constellation—individual yet interconnected, forming a map for future leaders to navigate their own paths.
